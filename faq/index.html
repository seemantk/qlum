Who?

Teachers might dedicate 5-6 hours per week creating high-quality quizzes. By sharing the fruits of your labor, you get to share the fruits of others' labor.

How?

Q-Lum works through participation. The pool of questions comes from teachers. Teacher, by providing, using and rating the questions improve the quality of questions.
  • Provide questions you've created for your students previously
  • Provide feedback for questions you download and use

Why do it this way? What about the experts?

The Common Core Standard has only reached adoption in only 38 states so far. As such, it represenets a new paradigm not only in testing but in teaching methodologies as well. The experts, hired by the publishers, are struggling tocreate one test per year so far. Q-Lum exists, then, to enable experiential expertise -- that is, expertise gained from the experience of adopting and using the new standard. That means: teachers like you.
